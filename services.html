Two Robbers is part beverage company, and part art project, created by twin brothers Vik and Viv Nayar in the heart of Philadelphia. Twice within a few weeks – no joke. Bolt lock blown apart. Cops said it was a professional job. We were *this* close to closing shop, but instead we doubled down.
